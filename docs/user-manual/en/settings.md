# Language and Settings
Where to change your preferences.

## Change language
Route: `/account/preferences`
- Open the Language section.
- Choose Auto, English, or Espanol.
- The interface will update based on your preference.

## Change theme
Route: `/account/preferences`
- Choose NeuroMode (dark) or Light mode.

## Manage plan and billing
Route: `/billing`
- Upgrade or change your plan.
- Access billing history in `/billing/history`.

## FAQ
Q: I changed language but some areas did not update.
A: Some sections update progressively. Refresh the page and continue.
