# Notebook
## Access
- Left navigation → Notebook.

The Notebook is your trading playbook. It stores rules, patterns, lessons, and improvements you want to repeat.

## How the Notebook is organized
- **Notebooks**: high‑level containers (e.g., “My Playbook”, “Risk Rules”).
- **Sections**: categories inside a notebook (e.g., “Entries”, “Exits”).
- **Pages**: individual notes with full content.

## Daily Notebook vs Custom Notebooks
- **Daily Notebook** keeps quick notes tied to your routine.
- **Custom Notebooks** are for structured playbooks and long‑term rules.

## Best practices
1. Keep rules short and actionable.
2. Update after weekly reviews, not every day.
3. Use the Notebook to document the “why” behind your rules.
