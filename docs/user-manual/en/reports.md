# Option Flow Reports

## Access
- Left navigation → Option Flow → Report.

How to read the Option Flow report and use it in your plan.

## Where the report appears
The report is generated inside Option Flow after you upload a file or screenshots.

## Main sections (plain language)
- Executive summary: quick takeaway and bias.
- Key levels: the most important strikes or levels.
- Aggressive flow map (ASK/BID): where the strongest activity is.
- Top contracts: largest premium or notable prints.
- Scenario matrix: possible paths and confirmation levels.
- Risks and notes: important warnings before planning.

## How to use it
- Start with the summary and key levels.
- Confirm with your own chart and plan.
- Send the plan to your Journal premarket section.

## PDF download
- Use the Download PDF button at the top of the report.
- The PDF is for educational use only.

## FAQ
Q: Is this a trading recommendation?
A: No. It is educational and must be validated with your own plan.
