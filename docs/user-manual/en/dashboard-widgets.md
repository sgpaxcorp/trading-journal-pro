# Dashboard and Widgets
Use the Dashboard to get a quick snapshot of your performance and habits.

## Customize your layout
- Drag widgets by the top handle to reorder them.
- Resize widgets to focus on what matters most.
- Your layout is saved automatically per user.

## Widget glossary
- Account Progress: compares start balance vs target balance from your Growth Plan.
- Green Streak: counts consecutive green days and shows green vs blue days.
- Checklist: daily actions and discipline checklist for the current day.
- Daily Target: shows today goal vs actual P&L when a daily target is set.
- P&L Calendar: monthly view of daily P&L.
- Weekly Summary: weekly P&L and day counts for the current month.
- Trading Days: year progress and how many trading days remain.
- Economic News Calendar: macro events by country.
- Mindset Ratio: compares sessions aligned with your rules vs sessions that broke them.

## Tips
- If a widget is empty, make sure you have saved journal sessions.
- Use the Daily Target widget only after setting a target in Growth Plan.

## FAQ
Q: Why does my P&L calendar look empty?
A: You need saved sessions in the selected month.

Q: Can I hide widgets?
A: Yes. Use the widget library controls inside the dashboard to show or hide items.
