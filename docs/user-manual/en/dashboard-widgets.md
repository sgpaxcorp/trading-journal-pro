# Dashboard & Widgets

## Access
- Left navigation → Dashboard.

The Dashboard is your daily control panel. It answers one question: **Are you executing the plan or drifting away from it?**

## Customize your layout
- Drag widgets by the top handle to reorder them.
- Resize widgets to prioritize what you need to see first.
- Your layout is saved automatically.

## Widget glossary (what each one means)
**Account Progress**  
Compares starting balance vs target balance from your Growth Plan. Use it to understand which phase you are in.

**P&L Calendar**  
Monthly view of daily P&L. Green = positive day, blue = controlled loss day.

**Weekly Summary**  
Shows weekly performance and day counts for the current month. Best for short‑term pacing.

**Daily Target**  
Shows today’s goal vs actual P&L when a daily target is defined in Growth Plan.

**Trading Days**  
Shows year progress and remaining trading days based on your plan.

**Green Streak**  
Counts consecutive green days and compares green vs blue days.

**Checklist / Trading System**  
Your daily discipline checklist. Use it as a pre‑trade gate.

**Mindset Ratio**  
Tracks sessions aligned with your rules vs sessions that broke them.

**Economic News Calendar**  
Macro events by country. Use this to avoid trading into high‑volatility events.

## How to read the Dashboard
1. Start with Account Progress to see your plan stage.
2. Check Daily Target (if enabled).
3. Scan P&L Calendar + Weekly Summary to confirm pace.
4. Use Checklist/Trading System to validate discipline before the next session.

## Tips
- Empty widgets usually mean missing journal sessions or missing imports.
- If your Daily Target widget is off, check your Growth Plan fields.

## FAQ
Q: Why does my P&L calendar look empty?  
A: You need saved sessions in the selected month.

Q: Can I hide widgets?  
A: Yes. Use the widget library controls inside the dashboard.
