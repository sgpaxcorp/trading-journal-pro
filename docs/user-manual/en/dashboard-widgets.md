# Dashboard & Widgets

## Access
- Left navigation → Dashboard.

The Dashboard answers three separate questions:
- `How is my account doing?`
- `How am I doing versus the plan?`
- `What did I actually do this week?`

## Customize your layout
- Drag widgets by the top handle to reorder them.
- Resize widgets to prioritize what you need to see first.
- Your layout is saved automatically.

## Widget glossary
**Account Progress**  
Shows the real account and equity state.

What it includes:
- `Current balance`
- `Net account change`
- `Return on reference`
- `Trading P&L`
- `Net cashflow`
- `Progress curve`

What it answers:
- `How is my real equity doing?`

How the reference is chosen:
- If a Growth Plan exists, it uses the plan starting balance as the reference.
- If no Growth Plan exists, it uses the first tracked equity point.

Important:
- This widget does not evaluate weekly, monthly, or quarterly plan checkpoints.
- `Trading P&L` is trading performance.
- `Net account change` can include deposits or withdrawals when cashflows exist.
- The progress curve uses the last 14 calendar days and shows ending account balance by date.

**Daily Coach Message**  
This is the short daily message near the top of the Dashboard.

What it does:
- gives one psychological focus for the day
- uses the daily message system
- stays separate from performance data

**Neuro Memory**  
This is the short reminder that comes from your recent Journal Neuro Layer inputs.

What it does:
- surfaces one repeated pattern from recent sessions
- warns you about recurring drift
- or reminds you of a stable strength worth protecting

What it is not:
- it is not a full report
- it is not a replacement for AI Coaching
- it is not based on one trade only

**Plan Progress**  
Compares your current balance against the Growth Plan path.

What it includes:
- current phase
- `Checkpoint start`
- `Balance now`
- `Checkpoint target`
- `Ahead by` or `Remaining`
- target dates

What it answers:
- `Am I ahead or behind the plan?`

Important:
- `Week / Month / Quarter` here do not mean realized P&L for that period.
- They are plan checkpoints.
- The system compares:
  - checkpoint base balance
  - current balance
  - checkpoint target balance

Example:
- `Week checkpoint start`: `$835.95`
- `Week checkpoint target`: `$855.88`
- `Balance now`: `$1111.83`

So:
- the required move from the checkpoint start was `$19.93`
- your current balance is `$255.95` above that checkpoint target
- that is why it shows `Ahead by`, even if today is red

**Weekly Summary**  
Shows the real performance of the current week.

What it answers:
- `What did I actually do this week?`

Use it to:
- validate real operating pace
- see true weekly performance
- avoid mixing realized performance with plan progress

**P&L Calendar**  
Monthly view of daily P&L. Green = positive day, blue = controlled loss day.

**Daily Target**  
Shows today’s goal vs actual P&L when a target exists in Growth Plan.

**Trading Days**  
Shows year progress and remaining trading days based on your plan.

**Green Streak**  
Counts consecutive green days and compares green vs blue days.

**Checklist / Trading System**  
Your daily discipline checklist. Use it as a pre-trade gate.

**Mindset Ratio**  
Tracks sessions aligned with your rules vs sessions that broke them.

**Economic News Calendar**  
Macro events by country. Use this to avoid trading into high-volatility events.

## How to read the Dashboard
1. Start with `Account Progress` to see the equity reality.
2. Check `Plan Progress` to know if you are ahead or behind the Growth Plan.
3. Use `Weekly Summary` to review the real result of the week.
4. Scan `P&L Calendar` and `Daily Target` to validate short-term pace.

## Tips
- Empty widgets usually mean missing journal sessions or missing imports.
- If `Daily Target` is off, review your Growth Plan fields.
- If `Account Progress` and `Plan Progress` tell different stories, that is not a bug: they answer different questions.

## FAQ
Q: Why does the weekly goal show as completed if I am negative today?  
A: Because `Plan Progress` measures your current balance against the weekly checkpoint in the plan, not against today’s result.

Q: Where do I see what I actually did this week?  
A: In `Weekly Summary`.

Q: Where do I see the real account state?  
A: In `Account Progress`.

Q: Why does the P&L Calendar look empty?  
A: You need saved sessions in the selected month.

Q: Can I hide widgets?  
A: Yes. Use the widget library controls inside the dashboard.
