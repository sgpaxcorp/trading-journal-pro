# Billing & Plans
## Access
- Left navigation → Billing.

Billing is where you manage your subscription and add‑ons.

## What you can do
1. Upgrade or downgrade plans.
2. Add or remove add‑ons (Option Flow, Broker Sync).
3. Switch between monthly and annual billing.
4. View billing history.

## Best practices
- Review add‑ons only if they fit your workflow.
- Keep billing cycle aligned with your trading timeline.
