# Global Ranking
## Access
- Left navigation → `Global Ranking`.

Global Ranking is the platform's **consistency leaderboard**.

It is based on:
- trophy XP earned across the platform
- number of trophies earned
- your public ranking visibility setting

It is **not** a profit leaderboard.

## What Global Ranking measures
Global Ranking reflects consistency and platform progress.

It rewards actions such as:
- journaling regularly
- maintaining streaks
- completing growth-plan milestones
- completing challenge milestones
- earning platform trophies

This makes it a better measure of disciplined platform engagement than raw P&L.

## What you see on the main page
### 1. Header
The header explains that the ranking is a consistency ranking based on trophy XP.

### 2. Your snapshot
This section shows your personal public ranking summary.

It can show:
- your exact rank if you are participating publicly
- your XP total
- your trophy count
- your tier
- your level

If your profile is hidden from the ranking, the snapshot will tell you that your ranking is hidden.

### 3. Top consistency ranking
This is the public leaderboard.

It shows:
- rank
- trader name
- tier
- XP
- trophy count

Only users who opted into public ranking appear here.

### 4. Public trophy profile
Click `View trophies` to open a public trophy profile.

That page shows:
- the user's public ranking snapshot
- earned trophies
- tier
- XP
- level

If you open your own profile, you can also see locked trophies.

## Tiers
The platform groups users into tiers based on XP:
- `Bronze`
- `Silver`
- `Gold`
- `Elite`

Higher tier means more trophy XP earned across the platform.

## Level
Level is a simple progression number based on your XP.

Level helps you see long-term progress even if your public rank changes.

## How ranking visibility works
Ranking is optional.

If `Show in ranking` is off:
- your public rank will be hidden
- you will not appear in the public leaderboard
- you can still view your own snapshot and trophy profile

If `Show in ranking` is on:
- you can appear on the public leaderboard
- your public trophy profile can be viewed by other users

## How to join the ranking
Go to your account/privacy setting where ranking visibility is controlled.

Enable public ranking if you want to participate.

## How XP is earned
XP comes from trophies.

Trophies are earned through platform progress, such as:
- creating and maintaining a Growth Plan
- journaling consistently
- reaching milestone counts
- completing challenge-related milestones
- hitting structured goals

## Important interpretation rule
Do not treat Global Ranking as a judgment of your value as a trader.

Use it as:
- a consistency signal
- a process benchmark
- a motivation tool

Do not use it as:
- proof of profitability
- proof of skill by itself
- a reason to chase trophies instead of process

## Best practices
- Keep your ranking public only if you are comfortable with it.
- Focus on process, not ego.
- Use your trophy profile to see where your consistency is strong or weak.
- Compare your XP progress with your actual discipline, not just your final results.

## Privacy
The public ranking and public trophy profile do **not** expose:
- email
- phone number
- address

They show only public ranking information and trophy information.
