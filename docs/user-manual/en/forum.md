# Community Forum
## Access
- Left navigation → Forum → Community feed.

The Community is a private space for logged‑in traders to ask questions, share progress, and review trades.

## How to browse
1. Use **Search** to filter by title or body.
2. Filter by **Category** to keep discussions focused.
3. Sort by **Most active**, **Newest**, or **Top (views)**.

## Creating a thread (quality standard)
1. Choose the right category (use “Platform Questions” for app issues or imports).
2. Title must be at least 6 characters and specific.
3. Post body must be at least 20 characters.
4. Describe context, what happened, and what you expected.

## Replies
Open a thread and reply directly. Replies support Markdown (lists, links, tables). Keep responses short and actionable.

## Best practices
- Share evidence (screenshots or steps) when reporting an issue.
- Keep feedback constructive and specific.
- Use the Forum to build repeatable process, not just outcomes.
