# Getting Started
Set up your account and complete the first loop in 10 to 15 minutes.

## 1) Create your account
Route: `/signup`
- Enter name, email, and password.
- Accept the terms and create the account.

## 2) Verify your email
Route: `/signup` (verification step)
- Open the email and copy the 6 digit code.
- Paste it on the verification screen.
- Use resend if needed.

## 3) Choose a plan
Route: `/billing`
- Pick Core or Advanced.
- Complete payment.
- You will be redirected to the app.

## 4) Complete your Growth Plan
Route: `/growth-plan`
- Define risk limits, goals, and rules.
- Save the plan before you start journaling.

## 5) Create your first Journal entry
Route: `/journal/YYYY-MM-DD`
- Open the date from the calendar.
- Fill premarket, in-session notes, and post-trade reflection.

## 6) Review your Dashboard
Route: `/dashboard`
- Check the P&L calendar and your streaks.
- Use widgets to see progress and daily actions.

## 7) Review Performance and AI Coaching
Routes: `/performance/analytics-statistics`, `/performance/ai-coaching`
- Explore analytics once you have a few sessions saved.
- Ask Neuro for feedback on a date range.

## 8) Option Flow (optional)
Route: `/option-flow`
- Upload a CSV or paste screenshots.
- Generate a report and send the plan to your Journal.

## FAQ
Q: I do not see data in analytics.
A: You need saved journal sessions in the selected date range.

Q: I am new, where should I begin?
A: Start with Growth Plan, then Journal daily, then review Dashboard and Performance.
